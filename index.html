About Me

Starting from a young age, technology has inspired me to try and learn everything i can. The internet allowed me to become a self taught java coder working on minecraft and other long lost minor projects. Bored with the pace of normal school i jumped into the job market working at a butane and propane extraction company named bhogart. There i helped with everything to sales and technical support to product design and marketing. I also begun my career as a technology consultant helping customers learn about solvent extraction, develop scalable Standard operating procedures and achive permiting in the state of California. After 4 years of Consulting i became passionate about blockchain technology. This pushed me back into learning coding and inspired me to go back to the fundementals. i spent about a year learning Go-lang and python before completing the UC Berkely coding bootcamp for fullstack webdevelopment. Now I continue to work on my passion projects which utilize the Ethereum blockchain.
